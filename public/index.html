About me

I’m Marguerite Champion, a PhD student at Inria Paris and the Laboratoire Jacques-Louis Lions (LJLL), working within the COMMEDIA team, which focuses on the numerical simulation of bio-fluids. My research is supervised by Céline Grandmont, Miguel A. Fernandez, Fabien Vergnet and Marina Vidrascu. I focus on the difficulties specific to contact in fluid-structure interaction context, motivated by application to heart valves simulations. My work explores how wall roughness affects contact dynamics.
